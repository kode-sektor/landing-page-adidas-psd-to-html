    Overview

    Adidas Femura is a new and multi-purpose design brand for athletes and hikers of all kinds. Creators, who love to change the game. Challenge conventions. Break the rules and define new ones. This is footwear that gets you to that proverbial finish line...and over. From low to high impact Women are not left out too. Feet comfort is not a prerogative; it's a sine quan non.
